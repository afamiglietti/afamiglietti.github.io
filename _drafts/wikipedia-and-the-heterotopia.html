Author's main question: "how can we understand [sites like Wikipedia] in their traditional role as continuation of the Enlightenment ideal, as well as situated and embedded in the distinctly different space of online networks?"

Massive oversimplification of actual Wikipedia editing process: "It describes itself as the encyclopaedia that “anyone can edit” [4], and this is exactly how it works. Anybody who is online can contribute to Wikipedia’s content and structure."

Only true for a very limited definition of "anonymous:" "The writers can chose to be entirely anonymous"
